# How The Hunt Works

## Step 1 - Sign Up

Sign up at [cicada3301.net](https://www.cicada3301.net/) and submit a public
Solana wallet address to track progress and receive possible rewards throughout
the hunt.

## Step 2 - Receive The Clue

Official clues are released through:

- [X / Twitter](https://x.com/3301coin)
- [The Clues Archive](https://www.cicada3301.net/clues)
- Other verified social platforms or clue domains

## Step 3 - Follow The Puzzle Chain

Each official clue begins a connected puzzle chain. A chain may include:

- Ciphers
- Hidden codes
- Audio clues
- Image analysis
- Book ciphers
- Logic systems
- On-chain traces

Solving the full chain reveals a key fragment.

## Step 4 - Validate Key Fragments

Validate each fragment through the
[Wallet Decryption](https://www.cicada3301.net/decryption) page.

Validation confirms that the fragment is correct and binds progress to the
participant account and submitted wallet address.

## Step 5 - Complete The Final Decryptor Hunt

After the required fragments have been released and verified, a final clue leads
to the master decryptor key.

## Step 6 - Unlock The Wallet

The master decryptor is entered into the Wallet Decryption page. If all required
fragments are verified, the decryptor unlocks the wallet material for the first
participant who completes the flow.

## Step 7 - Continue The Hunt

The campaign describes three wallets in total. Each wallet has its own clue
chain and final unlock path.
